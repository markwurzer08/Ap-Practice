Chemical Reactions

This topic covers how substances change during chemical reactions, including the breaking and forming of bonds to create new substances.

You’ll learn to identify types of reactions such as synthesis, decomposition, single and double replacement, and combustion. Balancing chemical equations is key to understanding conservation of mass in reactions.

Reaction mechanisms and energy changes during reactions are also explored to understand how and why reactions occur.
