Equilibrium

This topic studies the state where chemical reactions occur at equal rates in both directions, so concentrations of reactants and products remain constant.

You’ll learn about the equilibrium constant, Le Chatelier’s Principle, and how changes in pressure, temperature, and concentration affect the system.

Understanding equilibrium is crucial for predicting how reactions respond to environmental changes.
